# Sample Transformations

These examples are representative of the synthetic supervised task.

## Vendor coordination

**Source**

> I am writing to inform you that the vendor confirmed a delay in delivery and asked for revised implementation dates. We should keep finance informed so there are no surprises. Please confirm next steps by EOD Friday. Thank you for your attention to this matter.

**Target**

> Vendor update: the vendor is delayed and asked for updated implementation dates. Can someone confirm next steps by EOD Friday?

## Leadership update

**Source**

> Following our prior discussion, leadership requested a short summary of the launch risks and our mitigation plan. This affects leadership and should stay visible in the weekly update. Please confirm next steps before the Monday planning meeting. Kindly let me know if you require additional information.

**Target**

> Leadership ask: leadership wants a short summary of the launch risks and mitigation plan. Can someone confirm next steps before the Monday planning meeting?

## Finance operations

**Source**

> Please be advised that the invoice is missing a purchase order reference and cannot be processed yet. The current status has implications for the finance roadmap this month. Please confirm next steps this afternoon. Kind regards,

**Target**

> Finance note: the invoice is missing a PO reference, so finance can't process it yet. Can someone confirm next steps this afternoon?
